## What we do

Most of our engagements fall in one of these areas:

- **Performance and profiling.** Baseline reports, bottleneck analysis, optimization.
- **CI/CD and automation.** Pipelines that ship safely, deploy faster, and recover from failures.
- **Tech audits and modernization.** Independent reviews of architecture, code quality, and technical debt, with prioritized remediation plans.
- **AI implementation.** Practical AI integration into engineering workflows.

Full service breakdown: [dreaming-software.com/en/products](https://dreaming-software.com/en/products/)

## How we work

We pair AI tooling with senior engineers. AI scans 100k lines of code in hours; the engineer interprets the results, sets priorities, and stands behind every recommendation. AI didn't replace expert knowledge, it accelerated reaching the real problem.

We don't impose technologies and we don't lock you into our stack. We deliver measurable changes to your system, your pipeline, or your team's velocity. Not a 60-page report.
